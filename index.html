St. Helen's Stables

No Drama. Satisfaction.

A drama free resort for horses in the lush country side

At St. Helen's, we are focused on horse owners being able to relax in our friendly atmosphere. The safety and well being of our horses is our prime focus. We separate the herd based on age such as retired horses and younger horses. We have a variety of age, breeds, size, and gender. St. Helen's also raises cattle currently. We do not accept stallions. St Helen's is pasture board only but do allow temporary stall containment if need be. We have a relaxed group that get together occasionally. Activities include bonfires, riding events, obstacle courses, and other social gatherings (we're fond of archery!).
